# Rolling-Logs
Game Mechanic Prototype Developed With Unity.

Overview
---------
A Casual puzzle featuring a filled fish tank that needs to be transported without spilling. Four wood logs and a piece of board, thatâ€™s your dolly cart in the wild. Figure out how to safely get the fish to the destination by rolling the logs underneath the board on top of which sits the glass tank, such that the tank stays balanced. The less water you spilled the higher your score.
